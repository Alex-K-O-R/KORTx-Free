KORtx-Free Layers

Pic 1
Pic 2
Image 4
Pic 1. Yellow car. Pic 2. Black car. Image 4. Two cars.

KORtx-Free Layer is the collection of DOM-elements that are being changed by different triggers, but at the same time (as a result of specific single request). For example, every 60 seconds, request about current user role is sent to some entry-point. Depending on the result, some elements should become disabled, inactive or hidden.

KORtx-Free operates time's driven layers (TimeLay) and event's driven layers (EventLay). After specific of layers had been created with constructors, collection of elements should be described either by jquery selectors or by general data-attribute within every layer.

Collection of DOM-elements is represented by two types of nodes: NodeSync and NodeAsync.
